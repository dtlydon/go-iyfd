How to Play

Same as ever. Select the winner of each NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Tournament game -- as each round unfolds. Ignore those silly First Four "play-in" games on Tuesday and Wednesday, March 19 and 20.
You can enter more than one player. You can enter under an alias (like Joan Baez or Wavy Gravy. Other good choices: Sha Na Na, Canned Heat, Hendrix).
Or just use your real moniker. Your choice.
Make your picks AT THIS WEBSITE. Just go to the Menu and CLICK on PICK.
Click on the icons for each team you think will win. Once each pick is recorded, you will get a "Success" confirmation.

Score

Each correct pick will earn you:
  • In Round One: One point
  • In Round two: Two points

  • In the Elite Eight: Four points
