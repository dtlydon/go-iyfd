How to Play

It is the same recipe each year: Select the winner of each NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Tournament game as each round unfolds. Ignore those silly First Four "play-in" games on Tuesday and Wednesday, March 14 and 15.
You can enter more than one player. You can enter under an alias (like "Justin Wilson" or "Jambalaya Joe").... or use your real moniker. Your choice.
Make your picks AT THIS WEBSITE. Just go to the Menu and CLICK on Pick. Click on the icons for each team you think will win. Once all your choices for that round are highlighted, you will get a confirmation. Dat's it, boss!

Score

Each correct pick will earn you:
  • In Round One: One point
  • In Round two: Two points
  • In the Sweet Sixteen: Three points
  • In the Elite Eight: Four points
  • In the Final Four Semifinal games: Five points
  • For the National Championship game: Six points

Will be awarded to the top 7 finishers by total points, under the Official IYFD Payout Sliding Scale using quadratic equations with a smidge of calculus. There are no tie-breakers.
