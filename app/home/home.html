How to Play

It is the same kooky rap each year. Select the winner of each NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Tournament game -- as each round unfolds. Ignore those silly First Four "play-in" games on Tuesday and Wednesday, March 13 and 14.
You can enter more than one player. You can enter under an alias (like Lew Alcindor or Mrs. Robinson. Other good choices: Gen. Westmoreland or Denny McLain).
Or just use your real moniker. Your choice.
Make your picks AT THIS WEBSITE. Just go to the Menu and CLICK on PICK.
Click on the icons for each team you think will win. Once all your choices for that round are highlighted, you will get a confirmation.

When to Make Your Picks

PLEASE submit your picks no later than 11 a.m. on game days in the first four rounds, and no later than 3 p.m. on Final Four weekend: Sat, April 1 and Mon, April 3.
Those First Four “play in” games: IYFD ignores these. Just waste ‘em, man.
  • Round 1 picks are due: 11:00 AM on March 15 (Thursday)
  • Round 2 picks are due: 11:00 AM on March 17 (Saturday)
  • Sweet Sixteen picks are due: 11:00 AM on March 22 (Thursday)
  • Elite Eight picks are due: 11:00 AM on March 24 (Saturday)
  • Final Four picks are due: 3:00 PM on Saturday, March 31 for the Semifinals and 3:00 PM on Monday, April 2 for the Championship Game

Score

Each correct pick will earn you:
  • In Round One: One point
  • In Round two: Two points
  • In the Sweet Sixteen: Three points
  • In the Elite Eight: Four points
  • In the Final Four Semifinal games: Five points
  • For the National Championship game: Six points

Prizes

Cash will be awarded to the top 7 finishers by total points, under the Official IYFD Payout Sliding Scale using quadratic equations with a smidge of calculus and some Mexican weed. There are no tie-breakers.
